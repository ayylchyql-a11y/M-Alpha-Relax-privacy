隐私政策
最后更新：2026 年 7 月 3 日

一句话总结：M Alpha Relax 不收集、不上传、不分享你的任何个人信息。

我们不收集任何数据

本应用没有账户系统，不要求登录，也不向任何服务器发送数据。它不集成任何第三方分析、广告或追踪 SDK，不使用 Cookie，不获取你的位置、通讯录、相册或麦克风。

数据只保存在你的设备上

你的偏好设置、使用记录与统计等，全部以本地文件形式保存在你自己的 iPhone 上，仅用于让应用正常工作。删除应用即彻底清除这些数据；我们无法访问、也从未持有它们。

音频播放

所有声音均在设备本地实时合成，播放过程不涉及任何网络请求。

系统评分与外部链接

应用内的「给应用评分」「更多作品」由 Apple 的 App Store 处理，其数据行为适用 Apple 隐私政策。「联系作者」会调起你设备上的邮件客户端，是否发送完全由你决定。

儿童

本应用不面向 13 岁以下儿童收集任何信息。

政策变更

若隐私政策有更新，我们会在本页面公布并更新顶部的日期。

Privacy Policy
Last updated: July 3, 2026

In short: M Alpha Relax does not collect, upload, or share any of your personal information.

We collect no data

The app has no accounts, requires no sign-in, and sends no data to any server. It contains no third-party analytics, advertising, or tracking SDKs, uses no cookies, and does not access your location, contacts, photos, or microphone.

Your data stays on your device

Your preferences, session history, and statistics are stored only as local files on your own iPhone, solely to make the app work. Deleting the app permanently removes them. We cannot access them and never hold them.

Audio playback

All sound is synthesized locally on your device in real time; playback involves no network requests.

System rating & external links

The in-app “Rate the App” and “More Apps” actions are handled by Apple’s App Store, governed by the Apple Privacy Policy. “Contact the Developer” opens your device’s mail client; whether to send is entirely up to you.

Children

This app does not knowingly collect any information from children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, we will post the update on this page and revise the date at the top.
