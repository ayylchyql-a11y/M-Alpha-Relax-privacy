常见问题

为什么听不出双耳节拍的效果？
双耳节拍需要佩戴耳机（左右声道分别播放不同频率），用扬声器外放无法产生效果，App 内也会在未接耳机时提示。

锁屏后声音会停吗？
不会。App 支持后台播放，锁屏、切换其他应用都不影响正在进行的会话。

我的数据会被上传吗？
不会，所有内容都只保存在你自己的设备上。详见隐私政策。

怎么删除我的使用记录？
删除 App 即可彻底清除本地保存的所有偏好与统计数据。

FAQ

Why can't I hear the binaural beat effect?
Binaural beats require headphones (each ear receives a different frequency) — playing through the speaker won't work, and the app will remind you if headphones aren't connected.

Does audio stop when I lock the screen?
No. The app supports background playback, so locking the screen or switching apps won't interrupt your session.

Is my data uploaded anywhere?
No, everything stays on your own device. See the Privacy Policy for details.

How do I delete my usage history?
Deleting the app permanently removes all locally stored preferences and statistics.
